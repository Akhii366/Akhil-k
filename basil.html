Profile

Basil Joseph

I began my career in 2012 as an actor in the short film CET Life during my engineering days at College of Engineering, Trivandrum. I also scripted and directed the short films, with Albert S Oru Thundu Padam and Priyamvadha Katharayano during my stint at Infosys, Trivandrum I started my career in the Malayalam film industry by assisting Vineeth Sreenivasan in his third directional venture, Thira in 2013.My directorial debut was in 2015 with

Kunjiramayanam which had malayalam film actor brothers Vineeth Sreenivasan and Dhyan Sreenivasan acting together for the first time. My second directorial venture Godha was a sports comedy movie pivoted around wrestling got released in 2017 which had Punjabi actress Wamiqa Gabbi acting debut in Malayalam film.My third film Minnal Murali has Tovino Thomas plays the role of a homegrown hero with superpowers. The movie, which was announced in 2020 and released on Netflix.
